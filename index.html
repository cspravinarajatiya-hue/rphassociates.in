About the Firm

RPH & Associates — Peer Reviewed CS Firm

RPH & Associates is a Peer Reviewed Practicing Company Secretary Firm registered with the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI), dedicated to providing reliable, practical, and proactive compliance solutions to businesses across India. Headquartered in Rajkot, Gujarat, the firm offers comprehensive professional services on a PAN India basis, assisting startups, MSMEs, corporates, and foreign businesses with corporate law, regulatory compliance, governance, and advisory support.

With a client-centric approach focused on technical accuracy, timely execution, and strategic guidance, the firm helps businesses to navigate complex legal and secretarial compliances while enabling sustainable growth and expansion. At RPH & Associates, we are dedicated to becoming a trusted compliance partner for businesses of all sizes by delivering professional excellence, ethical practices, and client-focused solutions across PAN India.

About the Founder

Pravina Rajatiya, FCS

Pravina Rajatiya is a Company Secretary with more than 8 years of professional experience in corporate law, governance, and regulatory compliance. She holds qualifications of B.Com., LL.B., and is a Fellow Member of the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (FCS).

With a vision to simplify complex compliance requirements and provide practical, business-oriented solutions, she established RPH & Associates to assist businesses with reliable and strategic professional support. She is committed to the highest standards of professionalism, ethics, and client service.

She has completed specialized certifications including the Course on Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) and the Course on Goods and Services Tax (GST).
